## 2. ABOUT / HOW IT WORKS (expanded)

### What is Dripsta?

Dripsta is an AI-powered fashion rating and social styling platform. Users upload outfit photos and receive an instant AI-based style analysis, score, and actionable improvement suggestions. Users can compete on a weekly leaderboard and share their results on social media.

### Who is it for?

Gen Z and young millennials (16-30) who care about how they dress, love competition, and want honest feedback — not likes from friends who always say "you look great."

### How does the AI work?

Our AI scores every outfit across 4 professional styling dimensions:

- **Style Composition** — Proportions, layering, accessories, silhouette balance
- **Occasion Fit** — How well your outfit matches where you're going (12 occasions)
- **Color Harmony** — Color scheme detection, pattern analysis, metallic coordination
- **Trend Relevance** — Updated monthly with runway and social media trend data

The AI also generates a unique style label for every outfit and provides a body-region Style Map with specific tips for each zone.

### What occasions are available?

Casual, Work, Date Night, Night Out, Formal, Streetwear, Vacation, Athleisure, Brunch, Concert/Festival, Wedding Guest, School/Campus.

### How does the leaderboard work?

Every week (Monday to Sunday), users can submit their best outfit to the leaderboard. Your highest AI score of the week auto-ranks. Community likes contribute 15% to your final score, calculated Sunday night. Leaderboard resets every Monday.

### How does pricing work?

Dripsta gives you 1 free credit to start. Each outfit analysis costs 1 credit. Invite friends to earn more credits (3 per invite), or subscribe to Premium for unlimited analyses.

**Premium plans:**
- Weekly
- Monthly
- Yearly

Premium members also get 5x like power on the leaderboard.

### Common Questions

**How do I get more credits?**
Invite friends using your unique link. You earn 3 credits for each friend who signs up. Or subscribe to Premium for unlimited analyses.

**How do I delete a photo?**
Go to your Profile, tap the photo, and select "Delete." This removes it from your profile, the Discover feed, and the leaderboard.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Subscriptions are managed through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android). Go to your device's subscription settings to cancel.

**Why was my photo rejected?**
Photos may be rejected if the AI cannot clearly identify the outfit (too blurry, too dark, no clothing visible, or inappropriate content). Rejected photos do not consume credits.

**How is my score calculated?**
Your outfit is scored across 4 categories: Style Composition, Occasion Fit, Color Harmony, and Trend Relevance. Weights adjust based on your selected occasion. Community likes contribute 15% to your final leaderboard score.

**How do I remove my outfit from the leaderboard?**
Go to your Profile, find the photo, and select "Remove from public." This removes it from both Discover and the leaderboard.

**How do I delete my account?**
Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. All your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

**How do I report someone?**
Tap the report button on any public photo. Select your reason. Our team reviews all reports.
